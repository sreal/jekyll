100% Remote Work Tips

I live in Australia and work for a canadian company with a London & Montreal Teams.

Timezones. Learn then. Get a watch. Get 2 watches.

Be prepared for the internet to crap out. Have a backup.

When you're there, be there. You better be on skype, slack, hipchat, hangouts…

The routine is for your teammates. Routine make it easy to know when to contact you. Send a message daily, preferably at the same time.

Mobile internet is expensive. Not having it even more so.

Always know what you've done, doing, doing next.
