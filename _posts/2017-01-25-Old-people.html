Old people

Our lives are just a bunch of stories we've told ourselves so we can function 'normally'. These stories could be 'wrong'.

If you've told yourself the same story enough times, it's hard to tell when the story stops and we start.

At that point, if someone tells you the story is 'wrong', it's no longer about changing how we think about something. It's something 'wrong' about you.

If you're at the tail end, it makes sense that questioning one story becomes questioning all your stories.

That would be a tough.
